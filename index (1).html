STRATEGY INSIGHTS
🎯
High-Reach Content Types
Carousel posts get 3x more saves than single images — saves signal quality to the algorithm. Prioritise educational carousels every Tuesday. Reels under 30s get pushed hardest on Instagram right now. The viral and ASMR content should be under 30 seconds wherever possible.
🔥
Spicy Content Formula
The industry hot takes (Tue/Thu news posts) are designed to drive comments and shares — the two signals that trigger Instagram's distribution engine hardest. Don't be neutral. Take a side. "Most men's products are designed for women" type angles get reshared because they feel true.
⏱️
Best Posting Times
For Indian male audiences: Monday 7–9 AM (commute), Wednesday 12–2 PM (lunch), Saturday 6–9 PM (leisure peak). Schedule everything 30 min before these windows. Never post and ghost — stay online for 30 min after each post to reply to comments.
